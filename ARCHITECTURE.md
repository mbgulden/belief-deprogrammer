## Executive Summary

The Belief Deprogrammer becomes a **three-layer deconditioning engine** that maps Human Design open centers through two proven belief-work frameworks (Duzett + Nelson), producing personalized, comprehensive belief-reprogramming workbooks that address every level of conditioning from surface negative self-talk down to core identity.

What makes this novel: Human Design tells you **where** you're conditioned (open centers). Nelson tells you **how deep** the conditioning goes (tree model). Duzett gives you the **daily practice** to clear it. Combined, they create a tool that doesn't just identify beliefs — it surgically removes them and replaces them with empowering truths grounded in your actual design.

## The Generation Pipeline (Revised)

```
INPUT: Full HD Chart JSON (from OHDMCP)

STEP 1 — EXTRACT CONDITIONING MAP
├── Open Centers (2–9)
├── Hanging Gates per Open Center
├── Type, Authority, Profile, Cross
├── Variable (Motivation, Cognition, Digestion, Environment)
└── Not-Self Theme

STEP 2 — GENERATE BELIEF TREE (per open center × hanging gate)
├── Layer: FAULTY CORE IDENTITY (Soil)
│   └── 1-3 beliefs per open center
│   └── Source: Nelson's core identity model + HD center truth
│   └── "I am [center distortion] at my core"
│
├── Layer: FAULTY CORE BELIEFS (Roots)
│   └── 3-5 beliefs per open center
│   └── Source: Nelson's spreading belief web + HD hanging gates
│   └── "Because I am [core identity], I must [distorted behavior]"
│
├── Layer: LIMITING BELIEFS (Trunk)
│   └── 5-9 beliefs per hanging gate per center
│   └── Source: Nelson's central beliefs + HD gate distortion themes
│   └── "I should [specific behavior] because [conditional logic]"
│
└── Layer: NEGATIVE PROGRAMS (Branches/Leaves)
    └── 3-5 beliefs per hanging gate × 12 categories
    └── Source: Duzett category expansion + Nelson category integration
    └── "I'm so [judgment]. Why can't I just [impossible standard]?"

STEP 3 — MAP TRAPPED EMOTIONS
├── Cross-reference hanging gates with 60-emotion chart
├── Identify likely trapped emotions per open center
├── Generate emotional clearing prompts
└── Include inherited/absorbed variants

STEP 4 — CRAFT EMPOWERING COUNTER-BELIEFS
├── Personal design context injection
├── Nelson-style positive belief installation
├── Duzett-style present-tense positive phrasing
├── Gate-specific empowering truths
└── Not-self → Signature transition

STEP 5 — STRUCTURE AS WORKBOOK
├── 30-day Duzett format (5 beliefs/day)
├── Nelson tree order (surface → root per session)
├── Clearing modalities: scribbling, tapping, magnet/meridian
└── Processing/integration days built in
```
