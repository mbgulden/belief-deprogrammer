# Belief Deprogrammer: Human Design + Belief Work

## What This Is

A comprehensive belief-reprogramming workbook that combines **Human Design center mechanics** with **Allie Duzett's belief work methodology**. It targets the limiting beliefs absorbed through your undefined/white centers — the ones you picked up from family, culture, trauma, and environment that were never actually yours.

## How It Works

**Human Design** tells you *where* you're vulnerable to conditioning. Your undefined centers absorb and amplify external energy, creating beliefs that feel personal but aren't.

**Belief work** (Allie Duzett method) gives you the *how*: identify the limiting belief → clear it energetically → replace it with a specific, present-tense, positive empowering belief.

This workbook combines both. Each section targets one open center, with beliefs organized by source (environmental, generational, physical, mental, spiritual, subconscious, conscious, absorbed).

## The Daily Protocol

Based on Allie Duzett's "30 Days of Belief Work" structure:

1. **Read** the limiting belief aloud
2. **Feel** where it lives in your body
3. **Clear** it — scribble, tap, breathe, or use your preferred modality
4. **Read** the empowering belief aloud, slowly, three times
5. **Pause.** Let your body register the shift.

Do **5 belief pairs per day**. Each center section is ~150 beliefs = 30 days of work.
